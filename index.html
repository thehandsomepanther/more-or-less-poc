More or less is an idea about a new style of writing in which readers control their reading experience by opting in to the level of granularity at which they would like to read (this is done by either 1) adjusting the general settings above, which sets the verbosity level for the entire page, or 2) expanding on an individual sentence by clicking on its respective checkbox or the preceding text) .

This is a proof-of-concept that only uses CSS to perform the showing/hiding of text of various verbosities , although this site as a whole does use JavaScript to perform some DOM manipulations on page load (you can get a fully-functioning JS-free version of this site by saving this HTML document and ignoring the script tag at the bottom) .
